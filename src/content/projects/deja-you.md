## Overview

Deja You uses replayed player actions as part of its time-loop structure. The audio prototype gives older loops a progressively degraded sonic identity.

## Technical Focus

- Sample-level processing
- Low-pass filtering
- Delay lines and feedback
- Bit-depth reduction
- Gain shaping and smoothing
- Gameplay-state-driven DSP parameters

## My Role

I built the technical direction for the gameplay-driven DSP system and connected signal behavior to the design idea of memory, degradation, and repeated lifetimes.
