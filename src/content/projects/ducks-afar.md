## Overview

Ducks Afar is a story-driven puzzle game built around exact meals, strange resource packs, ancient machines, and readable interaction feedback.

## Technical Focus

- Puzzle-state management
- Gameplay architecture
- UI and game-feel feedback
- Audio feedback for puzzle clarity
- ScriptableObject-authored content and sequencing

## My Role

I lead the project and work across gameplay architecture, puzzle systems, implementation polish, and the feedback loops that help players understand transformations.
